Bhanu Kondabathula is an Indian UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer with a deep passion for creating visually engaging and functional user experiences. Endlessly curious and creatively driven, Bhanu has been designing since childhood—ever since he proudly hung his first painting on the wall at age 11. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Electronics & Communication Engineering, and during his academic journey, he actively explored the world of web design and front-end development. Over the past few years, Bhanu has designed websites for startups, events, nonprofits, and personal brands—each project crafted with care, creativity, and attention to detail. Currently based in Telangana, India, Bhanu works as an independent developer and designer, building clean, responsive websites with technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, and Bootstrap, while also experimenting with backend stacks like Java and Spring Boot. Beyond code and pixels, Bhanu enjoys branding, digital illustration, and creating 3D floor plans. Offline, you’ll often find him watching design tutorials on YouTube, sketching ideas, or spending time with loved ones.
